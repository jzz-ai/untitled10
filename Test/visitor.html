About Simply Rugby

A proud member of the Scottish Rugby Union (SRU).

Our History & Vision

Simply Rugby is an official rugby club based in Scotland. We have a complete tier structure from Junior Squads to Senior Teams, and are dedicated to promoting the teamwork spirit of rugby within the community.

The Importance of Volunteers

The vast majority of the club's coaching staff, medical support, and pitch maintenance is handled by enthusiastic volunteers (usually parents of players or retired players). To more efficiently coordinate our large volunteer team, we have implemented this new digital scheduling and coordination system to ensure every match runs safely and smoothly.

Safety & Compliance

All players are registered with the SRU for insurance coverage; guardian consent forms for all youth players are strictly archived in the system. We also conduct background and qualification checks on volunteers (such as verifying first aid certificates and coaching credentials) to ensure the safety of every participant.
